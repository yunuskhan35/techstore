    • About Us

    The Techstore is a web based application intended for online retailers. The main objective of this application is to make it interactive and its ease of use. Techstore is a project developed to make the purchase of electronics easy. This application makes the need of the sales and purchase of electronics safe, it also reduces the manual operation required to maintain all the records of sales and purchase.

    The user can then view the complete specification of each product. Selected products or items selected for purchase would be added into the virtual shopping cart. Which can be managed separately by customer. It can be examined at any time by customer for selected products, their quantity & price.

    The system allows registered users to login and new users to register. Then user can view the products, add to cart and buy the products from the website. The system will ease the shopping operations for customer of online store. It will provide vendor or administration functionality to manage categories and products. There are various functions and modules in this system to perform various features. Consumer will be able to browse and search products under different categories and compare them by sitting in front of the computer.
